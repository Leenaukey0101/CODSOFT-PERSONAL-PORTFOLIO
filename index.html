Rotract Club

The Rotary Club of GCOEN, nestled within the vibrant community of the GCOEN campus, serves as a beacon of service and fellowship. Coding Club

The Coding Club of GCOEN is a vibrant hub for aspiring programmers and tech enthusiasts alike. Within its welcoming confines, students immerse themselves in the fascinating world of coding, exchanging ideas and honing their skills. From introductory workshops on Python to advanced sessions on web development, the club caters to individuals of all proficiency levels, fostering a culture of continuous learning and innovation.

Driven by a shared passion for coding, members of the club collaborate on exciting projects that push the boundaries of technology. Through hackathons, coding competitions, and collaborative coding challenges, students not only sharpen their coding prowess but also cultivate valuable teamwork and problem-solving abilities.
